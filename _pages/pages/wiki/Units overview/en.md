**What is the Unit Overview?**  
The Unit Overview is a box that displays the units in all your cities.  
-> It is possible to select groups of cities.  
*You can enable/disable it in the settings.*

**Accessing the Unit Overview**  
You can access the Unit Overview simply by clicking on the small icon located above the "Go to coordinates" button. Itâ€™s the button with a small sword image.

**Using the Unit Overview box**  
Here is what the Unit Overview box looks like:  
![](https://grepolis-david1327.e-monsite.com/medias/images/units-overview-new.png)

To use it, just click on an image (when you do, the counter below updates with the corresponding unit).  
![](https://grepolis-david1327.e-monsite.com/medias/images/available-units-bullseye.png)

![](https://grepolis-david1327.e-monsite.com/medias/images/units-overview-button-horizontal-fr.png)  
Above is a menu that shows different sections:  
-- **Total** -> Shows all units available in your cities,  
-- **Available** -> Shows currently available units, excluding those sent as support outside,  
-- **Outside** -> Shows units currently sent as support outside your cities,  
-- **Support** -> Shows units currently supporting within your own cities.

You can choose to display the units from a specific group of cities.
