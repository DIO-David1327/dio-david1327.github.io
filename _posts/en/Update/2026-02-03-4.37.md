Dear Players,

After a year without updates, I’m back!!
I’m happy to announce the release of version 4.37.

<div class="gpcl note">Summary</div>

* The green color is back in window titles.
* Building overview filtering has been restored!
* Adds a button to disable the hero.
* The 15,000 limit in the cave has been restored!
* It’s too long to write everything — enjoy the read =D

<div class="gpcl tip">Changes</div>

**Window titles**
* The green color is back in window titles<br>
![Window titles](/img/update/Capture-d-ecran-2026-01-29-220413.png)

**Building overview filtering (administrator)**
* Following a Grepolis update related to the implementation of a filter, it became impossible to perform filtering via DIO-TOOLS.<br>
* This feature has been redesigned and is now integrated into Grepolis’ city group filtering.<br>
![Filtering](/img/update/Capture-d-ecran-2026-01-29-211136.png)

**Heroes**
* Adds a button to disable the hero<br>
![Heroes](/img/dio/settings/Heros.png)

<div class="gpcl bug">Bug fixes</div>

**Unit strength**
* If you had more than 10,000 soldiers of the same type in a city, “NaN” values were displayed. – Thanks to @Storekrog for reporting.<br>
![Unit strength "NaN"](/img/update/Capture-d-ecran-2026-01-29-212053.png)

**Cave**
* The 15,000 limit in the cave did not work; the total amount was displayed instead. – Thanks to @ReBoRnOAP for reporting.
* It was impossible to re-enable the feature after disabling it. – Thanks to @Aimin4strz for reporting.

**Building Overview Filtering (administrator)**
* Following the Grepolis update that introduced a filter, it was no longer possible to filter DIO-TOOLS. – Thanks to @Kajenje / @Optyfen for reporting.

**City name in battle report**
* It was impossible to click on the city name. – Thanks to @Nébu and @𝓐𝓹𝓼𝓽𝓻𝓲𝓴𝓮 for reporting.

**Script loading**
* Test modification of the script startup – Thanks to @kitbi2501
